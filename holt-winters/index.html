Holt-Winters Forecasting

Exponential smoothing for level, trend, and seasonality.

Description of Holt-Winters method.

Simple exponential smoothing $\alpha$

\[ s_t = \alpha \cdot x_{t} + (1 - \alpha) \cdot s_{t-1} \]
